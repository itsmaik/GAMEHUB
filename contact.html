FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Explore our FAQs to quickly find answers to the most commonly asked questions. Don't see what you're looking for? Contact us for further assistance.

How do I purchase games on your platform?

You can purchase games on our platform through the website by browsing the available games and selecting the one you want to purchase. Once you've made your selection, you can proceed to checkout using one of the available payment options. We accept various payment methods including credit cards and online payment platforms.

Do you offer refunds for purchased games?

Yes, we offer refunds for purchased games under certain conditions. If you experience technical issues with a game or are otherwise dissatisfied with your purchase, you may be eligible for a refund. To request a refund, please contact our customer support team and provide information about your purchase and the issue you're experiencing.

How do I download and install the games?

Once you've completed your purchase, you can download and install the games you purchased through your account on our platform. To do so, simply log in to your account and navigate to the 'My Games' section. From there, you can download and install the games you purchased by following the on-screen instructions.

Can I play the games on multiple devices?

It depends on the game. Some games allow for multiple device play while others do not. This information is typically provided in the game's description before you make a purchase. If you have any questions about a specific game's compatibility with multiple devices, please contact our customer support team.

What payment options are available?

We accept a variety of payment options including major credit cards, online payment platforms, and other payment methods. The specific options available to you will depend on your location and other factors. During checkout, you can review the available payment options and choose the one that works best for you.
